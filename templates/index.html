Motor Rent has been an established car hire firm in Sunderland for the last 10 years. When Andy started hiring out cars he only had a few second hand cars, but now the business has expanded to a fleet of 40 cars. Cars range from small 3 door vehicles to large 7 seat people carriers with a selection of automatic and cars with air-conditioning and navigation systems.
